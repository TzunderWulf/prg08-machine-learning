All assignments made during PRG08

Week 1 - Teachable Machine

First week assignment, working with Teachable Machine from Google. Very simple example, based on the hand sign you give, it responds in text. The model is very buggy, due to a lack of data it could train of.

See the live project

Week 2.1 - Pre-trained model with ML5.js

Second week we started working with ML5.js. The first steps with ML5.js was working with a pre-trained model. You give it an image, and it gives several results back. The results tend to not be accurate, but it gives some funny answers.

See the live project

Week 2.2 - Training your own model

Besides working with a pre-trained model, we also needed to train our own model. We do this by taking a bunch of pictures of a certain data (like wearing a mask or not). Then let it train and predict if we are wearing a mask or not.
